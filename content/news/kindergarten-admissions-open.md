Great news for prospective families -- **kindergarten applications for the 2026-2027 school year are now open** through [MySchools NYC](https://www.myschools.nyc/)!

## Why Choose TNS?

The Neighborhood School offers a unique educational experience:

- **Multi-age K/1 classrooms** where kindergarteners and first graders learn together
- **Progressive, project-based curriculum** that follows children's interests and builds deep understanding
- **Full-inclusion model** with ICT classrooms and comprehensive support services
- **Professional arts instruction** through partnerships with Studio in a School, Third Street Music School, and NYC Ballet
- **A warm, welcoming community** of families and educators who believe in the whole child
