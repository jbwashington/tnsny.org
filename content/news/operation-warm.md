TNS and Star Academy are hosting an **Operation Warm** gifting event on **January 15th, 2026 from 7:30 AM to 12:00 PM**.

Watch for a family letter and photos/media release form coming soon. If you'd like to donate a coat back to the school, please contact Denise at [denise@tnsny.org](mailto:denise@tnsny.org).
