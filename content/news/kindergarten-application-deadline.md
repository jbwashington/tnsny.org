Tomorrow, **January 23rd**, is the deadline to apply to kindergarten for children born in 2021.

**How to apply:**
- **Online:** [MySchools](https://www.myschools.nyc/) by 11:59 PM
- **By phone:** Call 718-935-2009 by 6:00 PM

For questions, contact the enrollment team at 718-935-2009 or email ESEnrollment@schools.nyc.gov.
