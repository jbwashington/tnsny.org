We are excited to announce that our annual **Spring Arts Showcase** will take place in May! This beloved event celebrates the incredible artwork, music, and dance our students have created throughout the year through our partnerships with Studio in a School, Third Street Music School, and NYC Ballet.

## What to Expect

- **Visual arts exhibition** featuring student paintings, drawings, sculptures, and mixed media from every grade level
- **Musical performances** by students from our Third Street Music School partnership
- **Dance presentations** choreographed through our NYC Ballet program
- **Classroom projects** displayed throughout the school hallways

## Save the Date

Details on the exact date and time will be shared soon via Konstella. All families, friends, and community members are welcome to attend.

## Volunteer Opportunities

We need volunteers to help set up the exhibition space, coordinate refreshments, and assist during the event. If you are interested in helping, please contact the PTA at [ptapresident@tnsny.org](mailto:ptapresident@tnsny.org).

This is one of the highlights of our school year -- a chance to see the amazing creative work our children produce when given access to professional arts instruction. We hope to see you there!
