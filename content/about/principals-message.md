## Welcome from Principal Dyanthe Spielberg

Dear Neighborhood School Families and Community Members,

Welcome to The Neighborhood School! I am proud to lead this extraordinary community of learners, educators, and families in the heart of the East Village.

At TNS, we believe that children thrive when they can build on their strengths. Our progressive, inquiry-based approach to education encourages students to ask questions, think critically, and make meaningful connections between their learning and the world around them.

Our school is a place where diversity is celebrated and every child is valued. Through our full-inclusion model, multi-age classrooms, and commitment to anti-bias, anti-racist education, we create an environment where all students feel they belong and can grow to their fullest potential.

I invite you to explore our school, visit our classrooms, and join our community. Whether you are a current family, a prospective parent, or a neighbor, you are always welcome at The Neighborhood School.

Warmly,

**Dyanthe Spielberg**<br>
*Principal, The Neighborhood School (PS363M)*
