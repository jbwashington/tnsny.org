## Support The Neighborhood School

The Neighborhood School PTA is a **501(c)(3) tax-exempt organization**, and your donation is fully tax-deductible. Every dollar you contribute goes directly to enriching the educational experience of our students.

## Where Your Money Goes

Your donations directly fund the programs and resources that make TNS special:

### 33% -- Arts Programs
Professional arts instruction through our partnerships with Studio in a School, Third Street Music School, and NYC Ballet. These programs ensure every child experiences visual arts, music, and dance as part of their education.

### 33% -- Teaching Assistants
Dedicated teaching assistants in classrooms across the school, providing additional support so every student gets the attention they need to thrive.

### 17% -- School Trips
Field trips that connect classroom learning to the real world, from visits to museums and cultural institutions to outdoor learning experiences.

### 17% -- Other Programs
Additional enrichment, school supplies, community events, and other resources that support our students and teachers.
