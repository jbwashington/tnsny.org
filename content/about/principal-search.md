## Our School Is Hiring a New Principal

The Neighborhood School (PS363M) is in the process of selecting our next principal. The NYC Department of Education posts principal vacancies throughout the year, and our community will play an important role in this process under [Chancellor's Regulation C-30](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/school-life/policies-for-all/chancellors-regulations).

**[View current principal openings on the DOE website →](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/careers/principals-and-assistant-principals/openings-for-principals)**

## What We're Looking For

NYCPS seeks self-motivated, visionary school leaders who can improve student learning by:

- Using data to set high learning goals
- Leveraging deep knowledge of curriculum, instruction, and assessment
- Developing staff and sharing school leadership
- Managing resources effectively
- Fostering a culture of excellence through personal leadership

### Eligibility

Applicants must hold a valid New York State certificate in one of the following:

- School Administration/Supervision (SAS)
- School District Administration (SDA)
- School Building Leadership (SBL)
- Administrator Certificate

*Internship certificates are not accepted.*

### Cover Letter Requirement

Applicants must address this question in their cover letter (100-400 words):

> Why are you an excellent candidate to lead this specific school, including any experience or special skills that you will bring as the new leader to enable the school to meet and surpass its goals?

## The C-30 Selection Process

The selection of principals is governed by **Chancellor's Regulation C-30**. This includes a school-based selection committee with representation from parents, teachers, and school staff. The process must be completed within 180 days of the posting closing.

## Community Input

We welcome input from our school community as we search for the right leader for TNS. If you have questions about the process, please contact the PTA at [ptapresident@tnsny.org](mailto:ptapresident@tnsny.org).
