## Our Core Values

While our formal mission statement is being finalized, these principles guide everything we do at The Neighborhood School:

- **Progressive Education** -- Learning through inquiry, exploration, and real-world connections
- **Inclusion** -- Every child belongs and is supported to reach their full potential
- **Anti-Bias, Anti-Racist Education** -- Actively working toward equity and justice in our school community
- **Community** -- Families, teachers, and staff working together as partners
- **The Whole Child** -- Nurturing social-emotional growth alongside academic achievement
- **The Arts** -- Creativity and expression as essential parts of learning
- **Social Justice** -- Empowering students to understand and improve the world around them
