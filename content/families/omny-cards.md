## Student OMNY Cards

TNS students are eligible for **free OMNY cards** that provide public transit access throughout New York City.

## What You Get

- **4 free rides per day** on NYC subways and buses
- **Year-round access** -- including weekends, holidays, and summer
- Works on all MTA subway and bus lines

## How to Get a Card

OMNY cards are distributed through the school office. If your child needs a new card, contact:

**Joann Tirado**<br>
[joann@tnsny.org](mailto:joann@tnsny.org)

## Replacement Cards

If your child's card is lost, damaged, or stolen, a replacement can be requested through the school office. Please allow **48 hours** for replacement card processing.

## Important Reminders

- OMNY cards are for student use only
- Cards must be tapped at the turnstile or bus reader (not swiped)
- Report lost or stolen cards promptly to the school office
- Cards are reloaded automatically -- there is nothing families need to do to maintain the card
