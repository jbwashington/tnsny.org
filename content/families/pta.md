## Every Parent Is a PTA Member

At The Neighborhood School, **every parent and guardian is automatically a member of the PTA**. There are no dues or sign-up requirements -- you are part of our community simply by being a TNS family.

## What the PTA Does

The TNS PTA raises funds and organizes programs that directly benefit our students. PTA funding supports:

- **Arts programs** -- Professional instruction in visual arts, music, and dance
- **Teaching assistants** -- Additional classroom support for every grade level
- **School trips** -- Field trips that connect learning to the real world

## PTA Events

Throughout the year, the PTA organizes community events that bring our families together:

- **Talent shows** and performances
- **Potlucks** and community meals
- **Ice skating** outings
- **Fall and spring fundraising events**
- **Welcome Back Breakfast** for families each September
- **And much more!**

## PTA Leadership

**Co-Presidents:** Elena Matt & Brooke Moreland

The PTA is led by an elected executive board and supported by dedicated committee chairs and volunteers. We welcome your ideas, energy, and participation at any level.

## Get Involved

There are many ways to participate in the PTA:

- **Attend monthly PTA meetings** -- Open to all families
- **Volunteer for events** -- Help plan and run community events
- **Join a committee** -- Website, fundraising, events, and more
- **Share your skills** -- Every family has something to offer
