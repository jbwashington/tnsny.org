## Daily Schedule

| Time | Activity |
|---|---|
| 8:00 AM | Doors Open |
| 8:15 AM | Breakfast Available |
| 8:30 AM | Morning Meeting / SING (Fridays) |
| 8:45 AM | Classes Begin |
| 11:30 AM - 12:30 PM | Lunch (varies by grade) |
| 2:40 PM | Dismissal (Pre-K & 3K) |
| 2:50 PM | Dismissal (K-5) |
| 3:00 PM | After School Programs Begin |

## Early Dismissal Days

On early dismissal days, all students are dismissed at **12:00 PM**. After school programs do not run on early dismissal days.

## Delayed Opening

If there is a delayed opening due to weather, doors open at **10:00 AM** and classes begin at **10:15 AM**.

*Schedule is subject to change. Please check Konstella for the most up-to-date information.*
