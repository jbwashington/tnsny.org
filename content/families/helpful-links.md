## NYC DOE & School Resources

- **[NYC Schools Account (NYCSA)](https://www.nycschoolaccount.nyc/)** -- Access your child's grades, attendance, and school information
- **[School Menu](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/school-life/food/menus)** -- View breakfast and lunch menus for NYC public schools
- **[NYC Department of Education](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/)** -- Official NYC DOE website
- **[MySchools NYC](https://www.myschools.nyc/)** -- Apply to NYC public schools (3K, Pre-K, Kindergarten, and more)

## Family Support

- **[Online Lunch Application](https://www.myschoolapps.com/)** -- Apply for free or reduced-price school meals
- **[NYC DOE Parent University](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/learning/student-journey/parent-university)** -- Free workshops and resources for families
- **[NYC DOE Support Hub](https://www.schools.nyc.gov/support-hub)** -- Get help with school-related questions and concerns

## TNS Community

- **[Konstella](https://www.konstella.com)** -- Our school's parent communication platform. Sign up to stay connected with your child's class and the school community.
- **[TNS PTA Facebook](https://www.facebook.com/TheNeighborhoodSchoolNYC)** -- Follow for updates and community posts
- **[TNS PTA Instagram](https://www.instagram.com/taborpta/)** -- Photos and updates from school events

## Additional Resources

- **[NYC 311](https://portal.311.nyc.gov/)** -- Report issues or get information about NYC services
- **[MTA Trip Planner](https://new.mta.info/trips)** -- Plan your commute to school
- **[NYC Parks](https://www.nycgovparks.org/)** -- Find parks and playgrounds near school
