## After School Enrichment

The Neighborhood School is fortunate to partner with a variety of outstanding organizations that offer after-school programming for our students. These programs provide enrichment in the arts, athletics, academics, and more.

## Our Partners

### The Honeysuckle Society
Creative and nature-based programming for young learners.

### Child's Play
Play-based enrichment activities for TNS students.

### Notes in Motion Dance Theatre
Dance education and performance opportunities that build confidence, coordination, and creative expression.

### AHN'S TKD NYC
Tae Kwon Do instruction teaching discipline, focus, fitness, and self-confidence.

### Intelligent Minds Chess
Chess instruction that develops critical thinking, problem-solving, and strategic planning skills.

### Hex & Co
Board game-based programming that builds social skills, strategy, and fun.

### AfroBrazil Arts Mindful Capoeira
Capoeira instruction combining martial arts, dance, music, and mindfulness rooted in Afro-Brazilian culture.

### 14th Street Y
After-school care and enrichment programming from the 14th Street Y community center.

### University Settlement
Community-based programming and support services for families.

## Enrollment

After-school programs are offered on a semester or annual basis, depending on the provider. Enrollment information is typically shared through Konstella and the school's parent coordinator at the beginning of each semester.
